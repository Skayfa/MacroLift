## Post-Interview Notes

**Immediately after each interview, document:**

1. **Profile**: Age, training experience, current goal
2. **Current solution**: What they use now, satisfaction 1-10
3. **Top pain point**: The #1 thing that frustrates them
4. **Feature priority**: Which feature they want most
5. **Price point**: What they said they'd pay
6. **Objections**: Main concerns/doubts
7. **Commitment level**: Hot/warm/cold lead
8. **Quotes**: 2-3 memorable quotes to use in marketing

**Analysis after 10-15 interviews:**
- Group pain points by theme
- Calculate average price point
- Identify must-have vs nice-to-have features
- Find patterns in objections
- Segment users (e.g., beginners vs advanced, bulk vs cut)
